Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, May 18, 2024

There are no minor details during preparation for the wedding. The quality of food, the professionalism of musicians, and design of the banquet hall should be at high level. Itâ€™s also especially important to choose the right colour combination for the clothes of the groom and bride.

When preparing for a white wedding you do not need to invent something special. There is almost always white dress for the bride and a black and white suit for the groom. But traditional ceremony opens up more possibilities for imagination.

In accordance with the Yoruba traditions, the families of the bride and groom can choose different gender colours of clothing. However, at the moment, many newlyweds deviate from these rules and create a single colour combination for all guests.

The wedding day is the moment when two families unite into one. The outfits of similar style and shades serve as an additional rapprochement factor from a psychological point of view. As a rule, two families at least wear the same headdresses to show that they have become a kind of united team.

The colour of the sky and the sea, cold and restrained but at the same time giving peace and tranquility - blue is the most "calm" colour for psycho-emotional perception.

In the cultures of many peoples of the world, various shades of blue symbolized eternity, constancy, spirituality, fidelity, stability, indestructibility. Blue was considered the colour of wisdom, higher knowledge, and spiritual power, so it was used to paint the walls of churches and temples, and the clothes of the gods were depicted in blue tones.
