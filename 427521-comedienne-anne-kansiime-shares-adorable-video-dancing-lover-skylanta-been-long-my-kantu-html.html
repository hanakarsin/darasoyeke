Comedienne Anne Kansiime Shares Adorable Video Dancing with Lover Skylanta: &quot;Been Long My Kantu&quot;

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Saturday, February 24, 2024
  • Kansiime and Sky have been serving couple goals since they hooked up in 2018, a few months after breaking up with her ex-husband Gerald Ojuok
  • The witty comedian recently revealed that despite her baby daddy being famous, she was not jealous of the groupies who throw themselves at him
  • The lovebirds have been sharing videos and photos bonding with their son, a clear indication that he has brought happiness into their lives

Award-winning Ugandan comedienne Anne Kansiime is happy in her new relationship with musician, Abraham Skylanta, aka Sky.

Kansiime and Sky have been serving couple goals since they hooked up in 2018, and they welcomed their baby boy Selassie Ataho, on April 14, 2021.

The 35-year-old mother shared a video dancing to Azawi's Slow Dancing song with Sky, and they looked happy. They showcased off their dance moves, and according to some of their fans, they nailed the #Slowdancingchallenge.

Kansiime talks about failed marriage to Gerald Ojuok

The funny woman parted ways with her ex-husband of four years, and she recently revealed that she does not feel guilty walking away.

"I don't think I feel bad or guilty about it. I am in such an amazing place right now, I can't regret what happened lastt year. There are some things that might have made me sad but...when you look at where you are, you should appreciate the story that gets you there, so do I feel bad I left my previous relationship? Not in a million years because I'm happy and very happy," she said.

Kneeling before her Sky

Responding to a question about if she kneels before her man, Kansiime said:

"Sky is my partner, he is not my husband yet but I kneel before him when it calls for it. When he comes home I don't kneel to greet him but I kneel sometimes and I'm not going to tell you where. Just know I kneel sometimes for my partner, it depends where."
