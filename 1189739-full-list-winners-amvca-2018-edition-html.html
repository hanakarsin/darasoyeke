Full list of winners at the AMVCA 2018 edition

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

The 2018 Africa Magic Viewers Choice Awards (AMVCA) which is the sixth edition was really a night for the stars both for the winners and nominees.

The award which is really redefining the movie industry and narratives across Africa was filled with many surprises and surprised winners. It was a night of drama as each winner came with one act or the order while receiving his/her award.

See the full list of nominees and winners below:
