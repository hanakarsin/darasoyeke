APC Commences Sale Of Nomination Forms

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Sunday, June 2, 2024

The ruling All Progressives Congress will commence the sale of Expression of Interest and Nomination Forms for political offices today (Tuesday).

APC had planned to commence the sale of forms last Saturday but was hindered by the inability of the contractor to deliver the forms.

Following the commencement of the sale of the form, the National Working Committee of the party has directed the organising secretaries of the party across the country to collect forms for aspirants vying for the State House of Assembly positions.

The decision was reached by NWC to meet up with the schedule of activities and the timetable of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC).

The National Secretariat of the party is expected to sell forms for the aspirants contesting for National Assembly, governorship and presidential tickets at three designated places.

The National Publicity Secretary of the party, Felix Morka disclosed this while addressing newsmen on Monday.

He said, “We are set to commence sale tomorrow (Tuesday). The guideline for the exercise will soon be released on our platform. While the forms for the State House of Assembly will be sold at the State level nationwide, others will be sold here at the national secretariat.”
