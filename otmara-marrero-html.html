Otmara Marrero - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Sunday, April 28, 2024

Television star who first became well known playing Izzy Morales in the Crackle series StartUp. 

Before Fame

One of her earliest acting roles was in the 2015 telemovie Instant Gratification. She also appeared in episodes of Ballers and Graceland. 

Trivia

In 2017, she joined the cast of the feature film Miss Arizona. She also appeared in the 2016 drama My Daughter's Dilemma. 

Family Life

Originally from Miami, Florida, she later settled in Los Angeles, California. 

Associated With

In StartUp, she stars opposite Adam Brody and Edi Gathegi.
