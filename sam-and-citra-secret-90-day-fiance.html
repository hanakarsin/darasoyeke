Sam of 90 Day Fianc Finally Told Citra His Secret

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Monday, February 12, 2024

Sam of ‘90 Day Fiancé’ Finally Told Citra His Secret — He Might Be Going to Jail

By Sarah Walsh

Dec. 18 2023, Published 10:10 p.m. ET

The Gist:

  • Sam had an opioid addiction in the past.
  • He got caught with loose suboxone in his car.
  • Citra wanted to go home once she heard his big secret.

As expected, 90 Day Fiancé took an intense turn when Sam Wilson finally opened up to his fiancée, Citra, about a secret that could potentially shatter their dreams of a happily ever after.

Sam's revelation centers around legal troubles stemming from his past struggles with drug abuse, which adds an unexpected twist to their journey. Keep reading to find out how this secret affects his relationship with Citra.

Sam admitted that he had a problem with drug abuse in the past, but he kept a secret from Citra.

Sam candidly revealed his past struggles with substance abuse. The 30-year-old, who battled opiate addiction since the age of 15, overcame significant challenges to lead a clean and sober life. His journey was marked by resilience, commitment, and personal growth. However, it has been hard to run from the shadows of his past.

Sam told Citra about getting caught with suboxone, but he lied about the paperwork.

In a shocking revelation, Sam disclosed to Citra that he had been caught with suboxone during a routine traffic stop. Suboxone is a medication used to treat opiate addiction. Even though he was prescribed the medication, he wasn't carrying his meds in the labeled container — which is a crime.

The lack of proper paperwork for the prescription led to his arrest. The judge offered Sam a way to avoid jail time, but he missed the deadline for filling out that paperwork!

As if the arrest wasn't bad enough, Sam admitted to lying to Citra about submitting the paperwork!

Citra wanted to go home to Indonesia when she found out.

The bombshell revelation had a profound impact on Citra. After learning the full extent of Sam's legal troubles, she expressed a desire to return to her home in Indonesia.

Citra's reaction was one of shock and uncertainty, which reflected the gravity of the situation. Despite her initial willingness to uproot her life for love, the uncertainty surrounding Sam's potential jail time raised significant doubts.

At the end of the episode, viewers were on the edge of their seats, wondering if Sam's legal woes were resolved or if the impending threat of jail still looms over him.

There is one thing we do know though. Sam and Citra are still together. Based on their Instagram, they are still in the U.S. — which means they did get married!

As the curtain falls on this chapter of Sam and Citra's journey, one thing is clear — their love story is far from over. The unpredictability of life, love, and the 90-day deadline sets the stage for a rollercoaster of emotions.

While the challenges seem insurmountable, we know that Sam and Citra managed to exchange vows before the end of their 90 days. With a handful of episodes left, we are all looking forward to seeing their wedding day unfold!
