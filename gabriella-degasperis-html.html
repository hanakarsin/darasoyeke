Gabriella DeGasperis - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Golfer famous for her success in junior tournaments, as well as her golf tutorial-driven YouTube channel which has amassed over 110,000 subscribers. Her content involves trick shots for golf and tips on the golf course.

Before Fame

She began competing in tournaments at the age of five.

Trivia

At 13, she shot a 64 during a tournament at Plantation Preserve. She's won multiple tournaments including the South Florida Junior Open. She's also an artist and designs her own logos. On Instagram, she has a following of over 230,000 on her gabbygolfgirl account.

Family Life

Her father Ron was her first golf instructor. 

Associated With

She has shared a photo of herself with former president Bill Clinton.
