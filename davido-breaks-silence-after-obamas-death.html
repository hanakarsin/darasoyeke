Davido Breaks Silence After Obamas Death

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Award-winning Nigerian singer, Davido, has broken silence on the death of his manager, Habeeb Uthman, AKA Obama DMW.

Naija News had earlier reported that Obama DMW died of suspected heart failure on Tuesday after he complained of severe pains.

It was gathered that he drove himself to the hospital for a medical check-up and gave up the ghost during the tests.

The development stirred reactions from important personnels in the music industry.

Davido while speaking on the death of Obama on Monday prayed that his Manager who is fondly called ’44’ Rest in Peace.

The ‘Fem’ crooner in a short video as he stepped out in Atlanta said “R.I.P 44.”

Obama was buried according to Islamic rights in Epe area of Lagos State on Wednesday.
