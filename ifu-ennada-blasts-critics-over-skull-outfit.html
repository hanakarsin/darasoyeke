'My Mission Was Not To Win Best Dressed'

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, January 1, 0001

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate Ifu Ennada has addresses critics of her costume at the African Magic Viewers Choice Awards (AMVCA) on Saturday night.

Ifu Ennada and her stylist Toyin Lawani received knocks from netizens over her skull outfit which many considered was too much for an award night.

In a post shared on her Instagram page, the reality TV star expressed appreciation to everyone who saw and understood the creativity and beauty in the skull outfit.

Taking a swipe at her critics, Ifu Ennada said her entire look is worth over a hundred dollars and anyone criticising her should have a minimum of ten dollars in their domiciliary account.

Ifu Ennada added that she does not please anyone but always minds her business and the mission for her skull dress was not to win best dressed but to bring Met Gala to MVCA.

She wrote: “First of all, I want to thank everyone who saw and understood the creativity and beauty in my AMVCA Transformer Look last night”.

“Now to those Cret!ns who wrote cr@p about my Look or intentionally posted my Look just to bash me, here’s what I have to say”

“My entire look is worth over 100k USD… Yes you read that right! So if you don’t have a minimum of 10k USD in a domiciliary acct, you have no right to speak on my Look. Basically, stay away from criticising my outfit if you’re a broke a$s”.

“Lastly, I am not one to please anyone, I always mind my business and I always do what I want because I really don’t give a fuqq about most people. I wanted to scatter everywhere and so far, I think I did just that, so if your existence hasn’t shook the internet at any point in your mis- er@bIe life, if all you do is sit in a god for s@kn cubicle and h8te on how people choose to live their lives, DO NOT SPEAK ON MY DRESS‼️”

“PS: The mission was not to win best Dressed, but to bring Met Gala to AMVCA and I DID JUST THAT”.
