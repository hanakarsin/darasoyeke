Cora Tilley - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Almeda Bohannan on Saturday, April 20, 2024

TikTok star known for posting content about her friends and daily life. She has had a number of TikTok accounts banned, including her former coratilleyyyy and coratilley accounts. She went on to posts to her former notcoratilley account, where she had over has 900,000 followers.

Before Fame

She launched her account in 2021.    

Trivia

She used the TikTok timer feature in a December 29, 2021 TikTok where she asked questions about her boyfriend and looked for angel numbers to confirm her questions as true. The video has earned over 8 million views. 

Family Life

She is originally from Georgia and has four brothers and two sisters. Her pet dog has featured in her TikToks. She has been in a relationship with Christian Hitchcock since December 2021. 

Associated With

In January 2021, she posted a TikTok duet featuring a clip from Lainey Heller where Lainey asked TikTok users to support Cora.
