Christian names for boys with meaning

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Check out our lists of Christian names for boys. Learn their origin and what they mean. If you are a young parent and you are searching for great names for your newborn baby boy, you are in the right place.

There’s nothing more exciting for young parents than choosing a name for their newborn. From the other side, it’s not only a pleasant chore but also a responsibility, because the name, as we know, stays with a person for their whole life.

If you are a religious person, there are lots of varieties to choose among Biblical baby boy names. They may be classic or modern, and each of them has its own meaning. Most people believe in the power of name and that’s the reason why the process of choosing one becomes a real challenge for parents.

Today, we have prepared for you some lists of boy names and meanings to make your choice easier.

  • Arthur. Origin: Celtic. Meaning “bear”.
  • Charles. Origin: French. Meaning “free man”.

  • David. Origin: Hebrew. Meaning “beloved”.

  • George. Origin: Greek. Meaning “farmer”.
  • Henry. Origin: German. Meaning “estate ruler”.
  • Jack. Origin: English. Meaning “God is gracious”.
  • Leo. Origin: Latin. Meaning “lion”.

  • Nathan. Origin: Hebrew. Meaning “given”.
