Outrage as Priest Kisses Young Girls on Pulpit, Video Goes Viral

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Monday, July 15, 2024
  • A reverend father in Ghana has attracted the ire of netizens after he was seen kissing his female students on the mouth at the pulpit
  • The actions by the unidentified priest got people talking on social media after the video was shared
  • Many social media users who watched the video condemned the act, with a section calling for his immediate arrest

A reverend father in Ghana has got social media talking after kissing his female students on the mouth.

In a shocking video shared on Instagram by Instablog9ja, the Christian cleric could be seen on the pulpit as the female students sat behind him.

The priest then proceeded to call the young girls one by one before kissing them on the lips.

As the kissing was ongoing, the audience members jumped up from their seats and screamed in excitement.
