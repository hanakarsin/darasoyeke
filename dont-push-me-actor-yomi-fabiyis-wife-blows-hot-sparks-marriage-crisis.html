'Don't Push Me' - Actor Yomi Fabiyi's Wife Blows Hot, Sparks Marriage Crisis Speculation

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Thursday, February 22, 2024

Grace Jimoh, the wife of Nollywood actor Yomi Fabiyi has called out her husband on social media insinuating that everything is not fine in their home.

This is coming barely three months after Yomi Fabiyi shocked fans with the news of his marriage to Grace, a student of his film school and the duo welcomed a son together.

Since then, Yomi Fabiyi has consistently taken to his Instagram page to share lovey-dovey videos and photos with his wife, Grace.

Yomi Fabiyi sometimes shared photos of himself and their new baby and expresses appreciation to his wife for staying strong throughout the period of her pregnancy.

However, Grace has fuelled speculations that their marriage is going through turbulence after some social media posts.

In the series of posts shared on her Instagram story, Grace called out her husband Yomi Fabiyi and issued a stern warning to him not to push her to the wall.

She wrote: Yomi Don’t push me o @Yomi Fabiyi, If you don’t want God to punish you.

Meanwhile, a check on Yomi Fabiyi’s Instagram page shows he has deleted all his wife’s photos and videos.
