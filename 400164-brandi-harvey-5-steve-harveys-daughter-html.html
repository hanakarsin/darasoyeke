Brandi Harvey: 5 little-known things about Steve Harvey's daughter

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Friday, August 16, 2024

Brandi Harvey is unquestionably the most popular of Steve Harvey children. She is actively involved in her father’s projects, especially running the foundation. In family photos on Steve Harvey’s Instagram page, it is easy to spot her. Even with her visibility, there are many little-known things about Steve Harvey’s daughter.

Having a celebrity parent means that most of your life will be in the public eye. Most aspects of your family, whether good or bad, find a way to the media. Despite such challenges, Brandi Harvey has grown to become an admirable person who focuses on helping others.

5 lesser-known things about Brandi Harvey

Brandi Harvey wears many hats. From an executive to an activist, she is actively involved in various fields. Despite having a rich celebrity dad, Brandi has managed to carve out her own path. These are some of the little-known facts about Steve Harvey’s most outspoken daughter.

1. Brandi has a twin sister

Over the years, the press and public have confused Steve Harvey’s twin daughters. Brandi is an identical twin to Karli Harvey. Born in 1982, the two are the eldest of Steve Harvey children. In most of his earlier specials, Steve Harvey made many references to being a dad to twins.

However, Brandi and Karli have had a complicated relationship with their dad. According to People, Steve abandoned his daughters to pursue a career in comedy. His choice of career broke the bond he had with Brandi and Karli.

In recent years, Karli and Brandi Harvey have mended their broken relationship with their father. For example, Brandi is the executive director of her father’s foundation, while Karli is often spotted in Steve’s tours.

Additionally, when Karli was getting married in 2015, Steve walked her down the aisle. The gesture was a public display that they have resolved their long family feud.

2. Brandi Harvey grew up in a blended family

Due to Steve Harvey’s many relationships, Brandi grew up in a blended family. Apart from her twin sister and brother, Broderick Jr, she is a stepsister to Steve’s kids from other marriages.

When Steve divorced her mother, Marcia Harvey, he married Mary Lee Harvey. The newlywed couple welcomed their son, Wynton in 1997. Unfortunately, the marriage did not last long. From this marriage, Brandi has one step-brother.

After being single for close to ten years, Brandi’s father married Marjorie Harvey in 2007. Unlike the previous relation, Steve does not have a child with his third wife. However, Marjorie came with three children — Jason, Morgan, and Lori.

For the past 15 years, Steve Harvey has tried to bring all his children together in a blended home setting. Although the TV star has been accused of negating Brandi and Karli from his blended family, the twins talk highly of their father. They share pictures of the blended family often to show that the family is intact.

Brandi is a vegan and an advocate of purposeful living. Although she has never shared what drove her to this unique lifestyle, Brandi has been vocal about her choice. Ms Harvey believes that her decision is both a statement for animal rights and better living.

A quick scroll through Brandi Harvey Instagram will give the impression that the business lady is passionate about positive living. For example, in an Instagram post she shared in 2018, Brandi was eating vegan ice cream. According to her, being purposeful with food and snacks should be everybody’s priority.

Besides being a vegan, Brandi is one of the celebrities to own entities, for teaching people about the lifestyle. When she was transitioning to be a vegan, there was very little information about the lifestyle. Fortunately, through her business, Beyond Her, she educates people, especially women of colour about purposeful lifestyles.
