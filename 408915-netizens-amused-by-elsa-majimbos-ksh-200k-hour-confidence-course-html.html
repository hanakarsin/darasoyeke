Netizens Amused by Elsa Majimbo's KSh 200k per Hour Confidence Course

- Content creator Elsa Majimbo has the world as her stage, and she is making money moves, one brand at a time

- The 19-year-old shared she will be doing a confidence course programme and interested people will have to pay KSh 200k

- Her fans loved the joke, but some were seriously talking about signing up for the course

Comedienne and internet sensation Elsa Majimbo is flexing once again, and this time around, she is giving people an opportunity to work on their confidence but a hefty fee.

The 19-year-old has been making moves with international brands despite the harsh criticism that she has been facing from Kenyans who say she is not as funny as people make her look.

Majimbo recently shared a post on her social media pages alerting her loyal followers that she is now taking in students willing to pay KSh 200k for training on confidence.
