Steve Harvey Reveals He Once Broke Up with Wife Marjorie When He Became Homeless

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Monday, May 27, 2024
  • Steve Harvey met his wife Marjorie in the 1980s during one of his shows, and he was smitten
  • They dated for a short while but broke up when he became homeless
  • They both married different people before reconnecting in 2005, a day after his divorce

American television presenter, comedian and actor Steve Harvey has opened up on breaking up with his wife Marjorie while they were still dating.

Speaking on the Carlos Watson Show, Harvey detailed how they met, leading up to their breakup due to his financial woes.

“I met her in the 1980s. I was performing in Memphis, Tennessee and she came to my show late. When I saw her I could not believe it. The audience started giggling because I was just staring at her,” he narrated.

According to Harvey, he swore to marry her one day in 1988. However, they broke up not long after when he became homeless.

“We broke up shortly after when I became homeless and I didn't want her to know,” he said.

Harvey divulged that after the breakup, they lost touch and they both wedded.
