10 Most Stylish Female Celebrities Of 2015 [PICTURES]

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Saturday, March 23, 2024

The other time, we did a quick round up on the 10 most stylish male celebrities of 2015 hereThis is a complementary version, a list showing 10 of the most stylish female celebrities of 2015.

[article_adwert]

The Nigerian entertainment industry owes a lot to the smart, glamorous ladies who strut the red carpets and liven the many late night gigs, especially as they have succeeded in marrying this glitz with relative career successes in an environment that easily cripples their efforts due to the glass ceiling phenomenon.

READ ALSO: Check Out Seyi Shay In What Seems To Be The New Fashion Trend

The elegance and grace of these women is noteworthy, if nothing, for the popularity boost our entertainment industry has gotten off their collective shine.

Quite a number of factors led to our compilation here, but chief among them is career longevity, their individual fashion tastes and influence on the Nigerian pop culture, brand endorsements, personal image, and lastly the zesty red carpet appearances.

In this light, we bring you 10 of our most stylish female celebrities of 2015 so far, ladies whose appearances have an overall influence on fashion culture in Nigeria today.

1. Rita Dominic

Rita Dominic takes the first place on this list because her sense of style is cool as ice and there’s hardly ever any off days for the light-skinned screen star.

2. Genevieve Nnaji 

Genevieve Nnaji follows closely with a glossy dark skin and a trim figure to kill for. She has an overall panache and fashion game that earns her a slot in any fashion list today, without a doubt.

3. Eku Edewor

Edewor is not one we should define because there are few words one can find to explain how she is effortlessly graceful and collected.

4. Omotola Jalade

Omotola Jalade is, in simple terms, the favourite celebrity’s celebrity as far as having a career and managing a family and kids is concerned. In addition, she has modish and chic so it’s pretty clear how she got in our list.

5. Mo Abudu

Mo Abudu has stayed plush and contemporary in terms of fashion and all round appeal over time.

In an age where looking skinny is the new cool, Abudu has defied the odds of age and the tall tales of going skinny to stay appealing. She remains an embodiment of the African woman with a balanced body frame and graceful qualities.

6. Mo Cheddah

Rapper/ Singer Modupe Oreoluwa Oyeyemi Ola aka Mo'Cheddah makes into this list for her fashion-forward looks and appeal.

7. Dakore Akande and Toke Makinwa share the seventh spot on this list.

Dakore Akande is impressive and reserved without being any less a style icon.

Makinwa is on this list because she deserves it.  Critics might say she is only a media girl and nothing more, but she is classy and sassy and everything in between so we overlook and acknowledge her as due.

8. Mercy Aigbe and Stephanie Coker share the eighth spot here.

Aigbe has a razor-sharp dress sense plus and great looks that do nothing short of please the aesthetic senses.

Coker's aura is noteworthy. You see her and you just know that you can put your money on her being elegant all day, any day.

9. Omowunmi Akinifesi

Akinifesi doesn't really need any introductions, does she? She is just a classy, young lady with all the powers to light up a red carpet and more.

10. Stephanie Okereke

Okereke is simple, yet bold and chic.
