Nairobi lawyer Karanja Kabage is dead

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, March 25, 2024

Prominent Nairobi lawyer and businessman Karanja Kabage is dead.

Preliminary reports indicated Kabage was pronounced dead at Nairobi hospital where he was taken shortly after his car veered of the road and hit a pavement along Southern Bypass, Nairobi.

The incident happened on the night of Friday, July 5. He was aged 70.

His family confirmed the incident though TUKO.co.ke could not immediately establish if he succumbed to injuries sustained in the the accident.

President Uhuru Kenyatta, who is in Tanzania on a private trip was among Kenyans who mourned the businessman.

He offered words of comfort to family and friends of the business magnate.

“It is painful to lose a great friend at a time like this. I pray that God will give the family, friends and relatives comfort and fortitude at this trying moment,” the President said

The president described Kabage as a resourceful person who was always willing and ready to offer legal aid to those who needed his intervention and help.

“Indeed Karanja Kabage was a resourceful person whose great ideas helped grow our insurance sector to what it is today. We have truly lost a great son of Kenya,” said the president.

Former Prime Minister Raila Odinga also mourned the lawyer saying his sudden death was shocking.

“It is with shock and sadness that I have received news of the untimely demise of lawyer, politician and businessman Karanja Kabage. My heartfelt condolences go out to his family and friends," said Raila."May God grant them strength at this trying moment and may his soul rest in eternal peace” he added.
