Rachel Hollis biography

Rachel was born on January 9, 1983, in Weedpatch, California, United States of America. The names of Rachel Hollis parents are Farrell Neeley and Sheree Edwards. Her father was a Pentecostal evangelist. She had a rough childhood that was full of trauma and lots of bad stuff.

Rachel has one sibling, a brother. The name of her brother is Ryan Neeley. She enrolled for her high school education in one of the local high schools in California. After graduating from high school, she enrolled at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in Los Angeles to study acting.

Career

Rachel relocated to Los Angeles to pursue her dream of breaking into the entertainment business. At the age of 19, she landed her first job as an Assistant at Miramax. In 2004, she started an event planning company known as Chic Events.

As an event planner, she planned events for many famous Hollywood actors and actresses such as Sara Rue and Bradley Cooper. Rachel also started the lifestyle blog known as TheChicSite.com that she still runs today.
