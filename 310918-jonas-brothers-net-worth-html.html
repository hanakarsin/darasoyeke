Jonas brothers net worth 2020: Who is the richest

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Saturday, February 3, 2024

Mirror mirror on the wall, who is the richest of the Jonas brothers? If you followed Disney stars in the early 2000's, then you know that Kevin, Joe, and Nick Jonas are a big deal. Years have passed and the boys’ lives have evolved, and so has the Jonas brothers net worth. Between the solo projects, the businesses, brand endorsements, and the reality show, it is exciting to see just how much the brothers have amassed for themselves.

The Jonas brothers are among the early 2000's Disney stars. They came into the limelight through Camp Rock, a 2008 Disney original movie. Two years later, they starred in a self-titled TV series Jonas, aired on the Disney Channel. Before hitting our screens, the brothers, Kevin, Nick, and Joe had been focusing on their music career. Are any of the Jonas brothers married? How much is Joe Jonas net worth? The details below will shed more light into the personal lives of these stars, as well as their value currently.

Jonas Brothers net worth

Forbes ranked the Jonas Brothers 40th among the Celebrity 100. At the time, the award-winning boy band reportedly took home 35.5 million dollars. Fast forward to nine years later, a lot of things have happened, including the breakup, the marriages, and the reunion. With several hit singles from their 2019 album, the Jonas net worth is expected to balloon. But, before we look at the cumulative wealth, let us focus on their individual value.

Kevin Jonas

Who’s the oldest Jonas brother? Yes, you guessed it right - the quietest and most laid back of all of them is. Kevin, now 32, is a fan favorite because of his reserved and humbled demeanor. The father of two stepped out of the limelight after the breakup to focus on rebuilding himself and raising his family.

During this time, he ventured into different businesses. Kevin’s projects include JonasWerner a real estate company and Blu Market, a company that focuses on influencer marketing. He also starred in Married to Jonas, a reality show that also featured his wife, Danielle. So, what is Kevin Jonas net worth? According to the Cosmopolitan, Kevin Jonas net worth is 20 million dollars.

Joe Jonas

After the Hiatus in 2013, Joe, 31, concentrated on what he knew best. In an interview with TimeTalks, Joe admitted that the breakup was hard on him as he did not know his identity outside the brothers brand. Nonetheless, he picked himself up and became the lead singer of DNCE a pop/ rock band.

The band released several hit songs including Cake By the Ocean that has been streamed over one billion times worldwide. The group won several awards including the 2016 MTV Video Music Awards Best New Artist and Teen Choice Awards party song of the year 2016. DNCE were supporting acts during the 2017-2018 24K Magic World Tour and Selena Gomez’s Revival Tour. Joe was among the 2018 Forbes 30 Under 30 Music Class. Reportedly, Joe Jonas net worth is 18 million dollars.

Nick Jonas

After the multiple wedding ceremonies, how deep do you think Nick’s pockets are? The youngest of the Jonas band members (27) featured in the 2016 Forbes 30 Under 30. He has often confessed that he is the one who suggested that the brothers part ways. After the breakup, he concentrated on acting and his music. In 2014, he released his album, Nick Jonas, that included the hit single Jealous. Two years later, he debuted his 2016 album Last Year Was Complicated. Nick has had three solo tours to date: Nick Jonas Live in 2014, Nick Jonas Live in Concert in 2015, and Future Now Tour (2016).

Nick also co-founded Safehouse Record. As an actor, he appeared in Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle (2017) as Alex. He will appear in the World War II movie Midway as Bruno Gaido, a radioman. Other solo projects Nick has undertaken include being a mentor on X-factor, hosting events, brand endorsements, and social media influencer gigs. Nick also has substantial investments in real estate. Reportedly, Nick Jonas net worth is $25 million.

Jonas brothers album sales

Following the announcement of their reunion, the band released their fifth album, Happiness Begins. The album opened at number one following the sale of 414,000 equivalent units in the first week. Sucker, a single from their fifth album that also features their wives (Priyanka Chopra, Sophie Turner, and Danielle Jonas) as video girls debuted at number one on Billboard Hot 100. The band’s portfolio also includes their 2006 debut album It’s About Time, the sophomore album Jonas brothers released in 2007, A Little Bit Longer, and Line, Vines and Trying Times (2009). To date, the brothers have sold more than 17 million copies worldwide.

Jonas Brothers documentary

Before the album, the brothers worked on Chasing Happiness, a documentary that captured their lives, including early days and Disney years. Through the film, they were able to address their issues, heal emotional wounds, and reconnect. As at 21st July 2019, the documentary had a rating of 8/10 out of 1,148 reviews on IMDb.

The most notable tours include Burnin' Up Tour (2008/2009) that grossed 41 million US dollars and Jonas Brothers World Tour 2012/2013. Their Happiness Begins Tour will start on August 7th in the United States and go all the way to February 22nd, 2020 in France.
