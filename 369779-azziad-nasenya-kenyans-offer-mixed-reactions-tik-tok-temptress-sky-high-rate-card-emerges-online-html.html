Azziad Nasenya: Kenyans offer mixed reactions after Tik Tok temptress' sky-high rate card emerges on

Posted by Hettie Henneman on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

- An expensive rate card has been making rounds on social media which is believed to be the rates at which online sensation Azziad demands as an influencer

- According to Azziad's card, a person or company will have to part with KSh 100,000 for a single post on Facebook, Tik Tok and Instagram

- Some went online claiming she was asking for too much money that they felt was not deserved while others supported her for knowing her value

Online sensation turned actress Azziad Nasenya appeared top on Twitter trends for the better part of Wednesday, July 22.

This is after a screenshot of her alleged rate card was shared extensively on social media leaving many stunned by the rates she demands for an online post.

According to the rate card, a person or company would have to pay KSh 100,000 for a single post on Facebook, Tik Tok and Instagram and about KSh 50,000 for Twitter posts.

A week's package costs KSh 250,000 while a monthly package for about two to three posts on each of her social media accounts would cost KSh 500,000.
