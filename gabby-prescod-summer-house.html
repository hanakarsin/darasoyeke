Meet the Season 7 Cast Member

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Monday, March 11, 2024

Who is Gabby Prescod in 'Summer House'? The newest season of the Bravo reality show features several new cast members staying at the house.

Although it's only the beginning of spring, things are heating up in the latest season of Summer House. The ongoing series on Bravo follows a group of friends who share a summer house located in Montauk, N.Y. Romance, scandal, and drama come into play as the friends try to navigate the trials and tribulations of their lives.

Season 7 features a whole host of returning cast members ready to let sparks fly at the titular summer house.

What is Gabby Prescod's job? Get to know the newest member of 'Summer House.'

According to her Bravo TV bio, Gabrielle "Gabby" Prescod is a professional fashion editor. The Trinity College grad started her fashion career interning for stylist Marie-Amelie Sauvé, per Guest of a Guest.

Regarding her personal life, she is reportedly very close to her family, who provided a necessary support system when her ex-boyfriend cheated on her while they were together. This is something that she boldly brings up with her Summer House companion Danielle Olivera, who was supposedly the one with whom her ex cheated.

Other than Gabby's messy love triangle, she is described as "single and ready to mingle." Personally, she has described herself as "a textbook Taurus who hates single use plastic!"

Article continues below advertisement

Gabby Prescod is currently the "fashion director at large" for 'Blanc' magazine.

Bravo TV also reports that Gabby is currently the fashion director for Blanc magazine. She has previously lent her skills to publications like Bustle, W, CR Fashion Book, Interview, and Grazia.

In an interview with Aurate New York — a sustainable fine jewelry company — Gabby dished on how her her lengthy fashion career has influenced her personal style.

Article continues below advertisement

"I remember when I just started working in fashion, I would dress in the style of the magazine I was working at. It took me a while to break out of that and dress how I wanted to but that’s not to say how I dressed then doesn’t heavily influence how I dress now," she shared. The fashion editor-turned-reality gal relayed that said journey is part of her "style DNA."

Gabby is also active on Instagram, having accrued over 18.6 thousand followers. She shares another Instagram with her sister, Danielle Prescod — a bestselling author and "15-year veteran of the beauty and fashion industry." The siblings use the account to promote themselves and their shared brand.

Article continues below advertisement

Gabby is one of three new cast members who joined the Summer House crew in Season 7. One of them is Samantha Feher. According to her Bravo TV bio, she is a hard-working, hard-partying content creator who is described as "very single." She isn't afraid to have more than a few male companions on speed dial until Mr. Right comes along.

There's also Chris Leoni, a former U.S. Marine who is currently pursuing a career in photography and filmmaking in New York.

See these huge personalities clash in Season 7 episodes of Summer House, which premiere Mondays at 9 p.m. EST on Bravo.
