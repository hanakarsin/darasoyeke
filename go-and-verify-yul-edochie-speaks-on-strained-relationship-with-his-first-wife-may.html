Go And Verify' - Yul Edochie Speaks On Strained Relationship With His First Wife, May

Posted by Chauncey Koziol on Saturday, March 30, 2024

Nollywood actor, Yul Edochie, has made a new revelation about his marriage, amidst rumours that his first wife, May has left their home.

Naija News reports that the actor while tackling a blogger who referred to May as his ‘estranged wife’ revealed that they are still living together in their home, in Lagos.

Recall that May celebrated her birthday on Friday, 17th September, with adorable photos shared on her Instagram page.

Just like Yul took to his Instagram page to celebrate her, actress Judy Austin also took to her social media page to shower prayers on the celebrant.

However, a blogger who reported the news wrote: Actor Yul Edochie’s second wife, Judy Austin, wished his estranged first wife, May Yul Edochie, a happy birthday on her IG page. Yul also wished her a happy birthday but the couple got no acknowledgement from May.

Displeased with the caption, Yul corrected the impression that his marriage with May has crashed and she no longer leaves in their home.

According to the actor, they still leave together in the same house and people can go and verify it.

He wrote: You know I have a lot of respect for you. This your caption no gel naa. An estranged wife means a wife who is no longer living with the husband. Both of us are still living in the same house, in our mansion in Ajah. So how is she estranged? Peter Obi will always say ‘go and verify. You didn’t verify this one before writing.
