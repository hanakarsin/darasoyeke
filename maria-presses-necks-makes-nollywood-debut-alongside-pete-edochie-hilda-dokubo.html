BBNaija's Maria 'Presses Necks', As She Makes Nollywood Debut Alongside Pete Edochie And Hilda Dokub

Posted by Enda Jantzen on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Former Big Brother Naija Housemate, Maria Chike Benjamin has delved into the Nollywood industry as she makes her acting debut with veteran stars like Pete Edochie and Hilda Dokubo.

Unarguably, Maria Chike Benjamin was the ‘star girl’ of the BBNaija Season 6 edition and has continued to use her controversial lifestyle to her advantage.

Despite spending only five weeks in the Biggie House, Maria has continued to bag numerous endorsement deals worth millions of Naira.

Apart from Whitemoney who emerged as the winner of the show, Maria is one of the housemates with over ten endorsement deals.

A video which surfaced online showed Maria at a movie set alongside Pete Edochie, Hilda Dokubo and others stars.

Maria posed with Pete Edochie for cute snaps and engaged with Hilda Dokubo in a conversation as the duo smiled at each other.

Many of her fans have expressed excitement over her new career choice.
