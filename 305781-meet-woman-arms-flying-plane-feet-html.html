Meet woman with no arms who is flying plane with her feet

Posted by Lourie Helzer on Sunday, April 14, 2024

- Pilot Jessica Cox's mother had normal pregnancy but she was born without fore limbs

- The first arm-less pilot faced challenges while growing up but she often pushed further

- Jessica became fascinated by the limitless ways the human body can adjust

- The 36-year-old woman developed passion to fly a plane but with her feet

Arizona-born Jessica Cox describes herself on her Facebook profile as the first arm-less pilot who believes in a future where people passionately pursue their dreams with resilience to overcome any challenge.

Since she was born without arms, Jessica became fascinated by the limitless ways the human body can adjust to a variety of circumstances and as a result, she developed passion to fly a plane but with her feet.

"I utilised dormant physical traits to adapt and use my feet the way people use their hands. As I learned to conquer physical barriers, I developed mental skills that allowed me to go on to achieve the seemingly impossible in my own, unique way," she said on her bio.

Her journey to inspire generations did not come easily as people often told her she could not do something because she had no fore limbs, but all these only motivated her to push even further.

“Being the first person to do this is pretty incredible, but I wanted to do this because it was about conquering my fear,” she told KTVK News.
